About AI Safety Officer

The mission, technology, and roadmap behind your OHS AI assistant.

Our Mission

Making Workplace Safety Guidance Accessible to Everyone

Occupational injuries and diseases cost lives, livelihoods, and economies. Yet accurate, timely OHS guidance has historically been locked behind expensive consultants, thick regulation documents, and slow bureaucratic channels.

AI Safety Officer was built to change that — to give every employer, worker, safety officer, student, and member of the public instant, plain-language guidance on workplace safety, backed by authoritative regulations and standards.

We believe that democratising access to OHS knowledge is a direct investment in saving lives.

For Developers

AI API Integration

AI Safety Officer is designed for easy connection to live AI models.

OpenAI

Connect GPT-4o or GPT-4o-mini.

AI_CONFIG.provider = 'openai'
Anthropic Claude

Connect Claude Haiku, Sonnet, or Opus.

AI_CONFIG.provider = 'claude'
Google Gemini

Use Gemini Flash or Pro for fast responses.

AI_CONFIG.provider = 'gemini'
Ollama (Local LLM)

Run Llama 3, Mistral, or others locally.

AI_CONFIG.provider = 'ollama'
OpenRouter

Access 100+ AI models via a single API key.

AI_CONFIG.provider = 'openrouter'
Local Knowledge Base

Default mode — no API key needed.

AI_CONFIG.provider = 'local'
